Local Foods: Seattle

Welcome to Seattle

Seattle has a very diverse food culture and is home to some of the worlds most exotic resturants. Here in the PNW we have the luxury of ulimited access to fresh seafood, fresh homegrown produce, and farm grown meats. Washington being a 'Green' state, you can always expect quality ingredients. You can find anything from Carribean food, to East African food. Seattle has been greatly influcenced by Asian culture, as you walk the streets it can be overwelling. especially if you find yourself in the middle of Chinatown.
